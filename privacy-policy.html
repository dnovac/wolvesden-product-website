Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

WolvesDen App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Team Selection: Your chosen local team preference for personalized content
  • Notification Preferences: Settings for game alerts and updates
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting our support team

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App features accessed, screen views, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Network Information: Internet connectivity status for data synchronization

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identifiers such as name, email, or phone number
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect payment information

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized team content and statistics
  • Send push notifications about upcoming games (with your consent)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Respond to support requests
  • Ensure technical functionality and troubleshoot issues

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with hosting services (Render.com) that help us operate our app
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Our backend servers use industry-standard security practices
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only

Push Notifications

Our app may send push notifications about:

  • Upcoming games for your selected team
  • Game results and scores
  • Important app updates

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app.

Data Retention

  • Usage data is retained for up to 2 years for analytics purposes
  • Support communications are retained for 1 year
  • You may request deletion of your data by contacting us

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of notifications
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. This privacy policy does not apply to those external services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or by posting the updated policy on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: support@wolvesdenapp.com
Website: Support Center

Contact Support

Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
This privacy policy was last updated on January 2025 and is effective immediately.
