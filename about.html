About TeamTracker

Built for local sports fans, by a sports fan

Our Story

TeamTracker was born from a simple frustration: following your local sports team shouldn't be difficult. As a passionate sports fan and software engineer, I found myself constantly jumping between different websites, social media accounts, and news sources just to stay updated on my favorite local team.

I wanted something simple, focused, and designed specifically for the mobile experience. Not a generic sports app trying to cover everything, but a dedicated companion for local team fans who want quick access to what matters most: upcoming games, live scores, player stats, and timely notifications.

TeamTracker is the app I wished existed when I was spending my Sunday mornings scrambling to find game times and wondering if my team's star player was still injured.

Our Mission

We believe that following your local team should be effortless and enjoyable. Our mission is to create the best possible mobile experience for local sports fans, cutting through the noise to deliver exactly what you need, when you need it.

Every feature in TeamTracker is designed with one question in mind: "Does this help a fan stay connected with their team?" If the answer isn't a clear yes, we don't build it.

Built With Care

TeamTracker is built using modern, reliable technologies to ensure a fast, secure, and stable experience:

We prioritize performance, security, and privacy in every technical decision we make.

Made by a Fellow Fan

Hi! I'm a software engineer with years of experience building backend systems and mobile applications. But more importantly, I'm a sports fan who understands the frustration of trying to follow a local team in today's fragmented digital landscape.

TeamTracker is my attempt to solve this problem, not just for myself, but for every fan who wants a better way to stay connected with their team.

Privacy First

Your privacy matters to us. TeamTracker is designed to collect only the minimum data necessary to provide you with a great experience. We don't sell your data, we don't track you across the web, and we don't bombard you with irrelevant notifications.
