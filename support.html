Frequently Asked Questions

General Usage

Go to Settings > Team Selection and choose your preferred local team from the list. Your dashboard will automatically update with your new team's information.
Make sure you have a stable internet connection. Data is updated automatically when available from official sources. If the issue persists, try force-closing and reopening the app.
Currently, the app is designed to focus on one local team to provide the best personalized experience. Multiple team support may be added in future updates.

Notifications

Go to Settings > Notifications within the app, or use your device's Settings > Notifications > WolvesDen App to customize your notification preferences.
Game reminders are sent 1 hour before kickoff. Score updates are sent shortly after games end. You can customize these timings in the notification settings.
Check that notifications are enabled in both the app and your device settings. Ensure you have a stable internet connection and that Do Not Disturb is not blocking notifications.

Technical Issues

Try force-closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, restart your device. If problems continue, please contact our support team with your device model and iOS version.
Stats are updated after official game reports are published, usually within 24 hours of a match. If stats are still missing after this time, please contact support.
WolvesDen App requires iOS 14.0 or later. Make sure your device is updated to a compatible iOS version for the best experience.
